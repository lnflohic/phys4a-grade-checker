How to publish your Google Sheet (Required!)
  1. Open your Google Sheet
  2. Click File → Share → Publish to web
  3. In the dialog, choose "Entire Document" or select your specific sheet
  4. Select "Comma-separated values (.csv)" from the dropdown
  5. Click Publish
  6. Copy the published CSV link and paste above

Note: Just sharing the sheet won't work - you must publish it to the web as CSV!
